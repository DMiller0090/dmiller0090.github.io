What is a marimba?

Marimbas are keyboard instruments. They have wooden bars suspended by string on hooks over tubes called resonators. The resonators help add warmth and depth when striking the bars with yarn mallets.

Types of Marimba bars

  • Rosewood
  • Padouk
  • Osage orange
  • Synthetic
